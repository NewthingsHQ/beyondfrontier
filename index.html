About us

Beyond Frontier was founded by Mr.Phumithep Klungurai, the vice president of the IMC Union of the International Mathematics Contest, with a goal of creating a love for mathematics in students, and developing their skills in problem solving. Beyond Frontier, along with Thai Talent Training, manages international competitions and the training of students to become rising stars on the international stages. We are also the first independent group in Thailand to bring students to international competitions.
